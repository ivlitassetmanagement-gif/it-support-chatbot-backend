MICROSOFT 365 EMAIL SETUP PROCEDURE

Step 1: Receive Welcome Email
- You will receive an email from the IT Support Team containing:
  - Microsoft 365 Login URL
  - Company Email Address
  - Temporary Password

Step 2: Sign In to Microsoft 365
1. Open https://www.office.com
2. Click Sign In.
3. Enter your company email address.
4. Click Next.
5. Enter the temporary password provided by the IT Engineer.
6. Click Sign In.

Step 3: Change Temporary Password
1. Enter the temporary password.
2. Create a new password.
3. Confirm the new password.
4. Password Requirements:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least 1 uppercase letter
   - At least 1 lowercase letter
   - At least 1 number
   - At least 1 special character
5. Click Submit.

Step 4: Configure Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
1. Click Next when prompted for additional security verification.
2. Install Microsoft Authenticator on your mobile phone.
3. Open Microsoft Authenticator.
4. Select Add Account.
5. Choose Work or School Account.
6. Select Scan QR Code.
7. Scan the QR code displayed on your computer screen.
8. Approve the test notification sent to your mobile device.
9. Click Next to complete MFA setup.

Step 5: Access Outlook Web Mail
1. Open https://outlook.office.com
2. Sign in using your company email address.
3. Enter your new password.
4. Approve the MFA notification when prompted.

Step 6: Configure Outlook Desktop (Optional)
1. Open Microsoft Outlook.
2. Enter your company email address.
3. Click Connect.
4. Enter your password.
5. Complete MFA verification.
6. Outlook will automatically configure your mailbox.
7. Click Finish.

Verification Checklist
- Successfully signed in to Microsoft 365.
- Password changed successfully.
- MFA configured successfully.
- Able to access Outlook Web Mail.
- Able to send and receive emails.
- Outlook Desktop connected successfully.

IF THE ISSUE IS NOT RESOLVED

If you are still unable to access your email account after following the above steps:

1. Visit the Productivv Ticketing Portal:
   https://productivv.ivlglobal.com/#/login

2. Log in using your company credentials.

3. Navigate to:
   IT Service Desk → Create Ticket

4. Select Category:
   - Microsoft 365
   - Email Issue
   - MFA Issue
   - Outlook Issue
   - Password Reset

5. Provide the following details:
   - Employee ID
   - Email Address
   - Contact Number
   - Detailed Description of the Issue
   - Screenshot of the Error (if available)

6. Submit the ticket.

7. An IT Engineer will contact you shortly and provide further assistance.

Setup completed successfully.
Your Microsoft 365 account is now ready for use.
