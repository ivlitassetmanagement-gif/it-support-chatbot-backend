PASSWORD RESET PROCEDURE
========================

How to Reset Your Password:
1. Go to https://account.microsoft.com/security
2. Click "Change password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Create a new strong password (minimum 12 characters)
5. Include: uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
6. Click "Save"
7. You'll need to re-login to all devices

Forgot Your Password?
1. Click "Can't access your account?" on login page
2. Verify your identity (email or phone)
3. Follow recovery steps
4. If still locked, contact IT support

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must change every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- No dictionary words
